Interior Walkthrough


VR interactive environment for interior design

The application uses a modern VR approach for walking around inside an interior. The main features include customizing object's materials runtime with the provided joysticks and interacting with pickable items. Made with Unity.

Aircraft Marshalling Simulator


VR Simulator for learning the basics of the aircraft marshalling.

The simulator has a custom 3D gesture recognizer system for recognizing movements made with the joysticks. Another interesting feature is the evaluation method for giving final mark to the practitioner, which is based on the correct signals with the joysticks. Navigating the pilot to make a good trajectory for parking the airplane is another evaluation. Made with Unity.

AR Watch


Try on application for hand watches with custom runtime reflections from the surrounding area.

The reflections on the watch are from the back and front cameras streams. For performance reasons, a custom combination of vertex and surface shaders is used, in order to get instantly the back camera feed as a spherical reflection on the watch. The front camera's photo is taken only once at startup of the app and is merged with the live sphercical reflection from the back camera, so that you can see your face in the watch, when it is pointed to you as looking into a mirror. Made with Unity.
